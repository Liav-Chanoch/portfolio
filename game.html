TriShift

A strategy game on a 4×4 board. The symbol you place shifts every turn — so you must plan ahead, block your opponent, and sometimes use a symbol you didn't want. First to land 3 in a row wins.

Playing
Up next
01 — The board A 4×4 grid. Two players take turns clicking to place a symbol.
02 — The symbols Three symbols cycle each turn: ✕, ○, △. You don't choose — you get whatever is next.
03 — How to win Get 3 of the same symbol in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The player who places the 3rd matching symbol wins.
04 — The twist Since symbols cycle for both players, you might unknowingly set up your opponent — or block them by filling a key cell with an off-symbol.
